Welcome to Cloud Jumper!

Game Play

Use the left and right arrow keys to help Kirby jump as high into the sky as he can by bouncing on the clouds. If you fall off the screen then you lose.

  • Gives you 100 points & a higher jump
  • Gives you 60 points & a shorter jump
  • Gives you an extra 1000 points!

Enemies

  • Avoid Bandana Waddle Dees. Hit one and you lose.
