Welcome to Cloud Jumper!

Use the left and right arrow keys to help Kirby jump as high into the sky as he can by bouncing on the clouds. Be careful for Bandana Waddle Dees along the way. If you hit them or fall below the screen then it's game over. The white clouds will give Kirby an extra boost (and points) while the gray clouds will hinder his jump speed.

If you see a star along the way make sure to grab them and you'll get an extra 1000 points!

Press start and see how many points you can get!
