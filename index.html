Welcome to Cloud Jumper!

Your goal is to see Kirby jumping up into the sky by landing on clouds as he moves up. The white clouds will give Kirby an extra boost (and points) while the gray clouds will hinder his jump speed. If you see a star along the way make sure to grab them and you'll get an extra 1000 points! Watch out for Bandana Waddle Dees who might be hiding along the way. If you hit them it's game over. Press start and see how many points you can get!
